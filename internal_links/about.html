About ISA

The mission of ISA is to voice the concerns and needs of the international student body at USC as well as provide a platform for showcasing the diverse cultures on campus. We aim to create a cultural exchange at USC, giving American students a chance to get a taste of various cultures and giving international students a chance to experience life in the United States. It is our hope to create connections between the diverse students on campus to build a stronger Trojan family at USC.
